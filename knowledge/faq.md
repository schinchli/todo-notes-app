# Frequently asked questions

## How does the daily email digest work?

Open Settings, confirm your email address, and enable the digest. Every day at
8 AM (Asia/Kolkata) the app emails you a summary of notes that are overdue or
due within the next 24 hours. Notes without a due date are never included.
Use Send test digest to verify delivery immediately after saving settings.

## What can the AI assistant do?

The assistant can search your notes, list what is due soon, create new notes,
and mark notes complete. Actions that change your data (creating or completing
notes) always ask for your approval first — you can choose Yes, No, or Trust to
stop being asked for the rest of the conversation.

## Is my data private?

Notes are stored per user. You can only ever see, search, or modify your own
notes, and the assistant's tools are scoped to your account.

## Why is there a due-date reminder but no push notification?

Reminders are delivered by email only. Locally (during development) emails are
captured to the console instead of being sent.

## How do I reset my password?

Password reset requires code delivery to be configured. In the demo build,
create a new account or ask the administrator to reset your account.
